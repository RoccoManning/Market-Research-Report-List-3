<p><p>The Lithium Battery Material market consists of two main types: Cathode Material and Anode Material. Cathode Material refers to the positive electrode in the battery, typically made of materials like lithium cobalt oxide or lithium iron phosphate. Anode Material, on the other hand, is the negative electrode, usually composed of materials like graphite or silicon. Both types play a crucial role in the functioning of lithium batteries by facilitating the movement of lithium ions between the electrodes during charging and discharging.</p></p>
