<p><p>Pipe coatings market includes liquid and powder coatings. Liquid coatings are applied in a wet form and are often used for pipelines that require a thicker layer of protection. On the other hand, powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and then melted to form a smooth, durable finish. Powder coatings are known for their resistance to chemicals, corrosion, and impact. Both liquid and powder coatings offer different advantages depending on the specific requirements of the pipeline and environmental conditions.</p></p>
