<p><p>Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) headphones come in three main market types - in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear. In-ear headphones fit snugly inside the ear canal, creating a seal that blocks external noise. They are compact and portable, making them suitable for active use. On-ear headphones rest on the ears, covering them partially, and provide a more comfortable fit. These headphones are lightweight and offer good noise cancellation. Over-ear headphones fully enclose the ears, providing the best noise isolation. They offer superior sound quality and are comfortable for extended wear.</p></p>
