<p><p>Terahertz imaging detection is a technology used to detect and capture images using terahertz waves, which lie between the microwave and infrared spectra. The market for terahertz imaging detection can be divided into two types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging relies on natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, allowing for non-intrusive imaging. Active terahertz imaging, on the other hand, involves the use of terahertz sources to illuminate objects, providing better control and higher image resolution. Both types find applications in areas such as security screening, medical imaging, and material characterization.</p></p>
