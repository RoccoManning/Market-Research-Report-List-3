<p><p>The flaxseed oil nutrition market can be classified into three types based on the extraction methods: squeezing method, hot pressing method, and leaching method. The squeezing method involves mechanical pressing of flaxseeds to extract oil, preserving its natural nutrients. The hot pressing method involves applying heat during the pressing process to increase oil yield but may reduce nutritional content. The leaching method involves using solvents to extract oil from flaxseeds, which may result in lower nutritional quality due to the chemical extraction process.</p></p>
