<p><ul><li>Peristaltic Pump System</li><li>Syringe Pump System</li><li>Automated On-Line DT-LC System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolution testers are used for evaluating the dissolution rates of solid dosage forms in different mediums. The three main types of dissolution testers are the Peristaltic Pump System, Syringe Pump System, and Automated On-Line DT-LC System. The Peristaltic Pump System operates by using a roller to squeeze a tube containing the dissolution medium, delivering it to the vessel. The Syringe Pump System uses a motor-driven syringe to deliver the medium. The Automated On-Line DT-LC System combines dissolution testing with liquid chromatography, allowing for real-time analysis of the sample.</p></p>
