<p><p>The manual binding machine market comprises two main types: tabletop binding machines and portable binding machines. Tabletop binding machines are designed for heavy-duty use and are commonly found in office settings. They offer a higher binding capacity and can handle larger volumes of documents. On the other hand, portable binding machines are compact and lightweight, making them suitable for on-the-go use. They are often used by freelancers or professionals who frequently travel or work from different locations. Both types of machines offer manual operation, allowing individuals to manually punch holes and bind documents without the need for electricity.</p></p>
