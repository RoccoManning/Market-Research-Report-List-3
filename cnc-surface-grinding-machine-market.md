Horizontal machines have a horizontal spindle orientation, offering better stability and precision for grinding larger and heavier workpieces. Vertical machines, on the other hand, have a vertical spindle orientation, making them suitable for grinding smaller workpieces or parts with complex shapes. Both types are widely used in various industries for achieving high-quality surface finishes and accurate dimensions on metal, plastic, or composite materials.</p></p>
