<p><p>Acetylene gas is produced through two main market types - calcium carbide production type and hydrocarbon pyrolysis production type. In the calcium carbide production type, acetylene gas is generated by reacting calcium carbide with water. On the other hand, hydrocarbon pyrolysis production type involves the thermal cracking of hydrocarbons to form acetylene gas. Both processes are widely used in industrial applications and have their own advantages and disadvantages in terms of production efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
