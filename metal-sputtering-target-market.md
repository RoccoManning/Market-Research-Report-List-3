<p><p>The metal sputtering target market includes two main types: metal targets and alloy targets. Metal targets refer to sputtering targets made from a single type of metal, such as aluminum, gold, or titanium. They are used for depositing a thin layer of pure metal onto a substrate in various applications. On the other hand, alloy targets are composed of multiple metals combined in specific ratios. These targets are used when a film with specific alloy composition is required, offering a wider range of material properties for different applications.</p></p>
