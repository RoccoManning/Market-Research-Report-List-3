<p><strong>The Drum Brake System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Leading Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Leading Shoe Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum brake systems are categorized into three main types based on the arrangement of the brake shoes within the drum. Leading Trailing Shoe Brake has one leading shoe and one trailing shoe, with the leading shoe making contact with the drum first. Dual Two Trailing Shoe Brake consists of two trailing shoes on each side of the drum. Dual Two Leading Shoe Brake has two leading shoes on each side. Each type of system has its own unique design and advantages in terms of braking performance and efficiency.</p></p>
