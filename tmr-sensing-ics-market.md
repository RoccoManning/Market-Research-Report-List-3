<p><p>TMR Sensing ICs, or Tunnel Magnetoresistive Sensing Integrated Circuits, come in two market types: Analog and Digital. Analog TMR Sensing ICs convert magnetic field variations into continuous output voltage signals, making them suitable for applications requiring precise measurement and sensing. On the other hand, Digital TMR Sensing ICs process the magnetic field variations into discrete digital signals, making them ideal for applications that require binary information or digital communication. These market types cater to different needs depending on the desired output and application requirements.</p></p>
