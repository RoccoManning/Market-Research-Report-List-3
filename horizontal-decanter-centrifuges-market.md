<p><p>The Horizontal Decanter Centrifuges market consists of two types: two-phase decanter centrifuge and three-phase decanter centrifuge. A two-phase decanter centrifuge is primarily used for solid-liquid separation, where it separates the solid particles from the liquid phase. On the other hand, a three-phase decanter centrifuge is capable of separating two immiscible liquid phases along with solid particles. Both types find applications in various industries such as wastewater treatment, chemical, and food processing, enabling efficient separation processes.</p></p>
