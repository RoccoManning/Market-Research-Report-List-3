<p><p>Downeast motor yachts are available in three market types: planing hull, displacement hull, and semi-displacement hull. A planing hull allows the yacht to lift partially out of the water and reach higher speeds, ideal for those who prioritize speed and performance. A displacement hull, on the other hand, is designed for stability and fuel efficiency, allowing for long-distance cruising at slower speeds. Lastly, a semi-displacement hull combines the benefits of both planing and displacement hulls, enabling moderate speeds with improved fuel efficiency and stability.</p></p>
