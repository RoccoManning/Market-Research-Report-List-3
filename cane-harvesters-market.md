<p><p>Cane harvesters are machines used to mechanically harvest sugarcane. There are two main types of cane harvesters in the market, namely, wheel harvesters and crawler harvesters. Wheel harvesters are equipped with large wheels and are suitable for flat terrains. They move quickly between rows of cane, efficiently cutting and loading the cane into their bins. On the other hand, crawler harvesters have tracks instead of wheels, allowing them to navigate rough and muddy terrains. They have higher stability but may move at a slower pace compared to wheel harvesters.</p></p>
