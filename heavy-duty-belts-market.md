<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The heavy-duty belts market comprises three types: multiply fabric belts, steel cord belts, and solid woven belts. Multiply fabric belts are made by layering several fabric plies, providing high strength and flexibility. Steel cord belts use steel cords embedded in rubber to offer excellent tensile strength and resistance to impact. Solid woven belts are built with a single layer of solid woven fabric, delivering durability and abrasion resistance. These different types of heavy-duty belts cater to various industries and applications, such as mining, construction, and material handling.</p></p>
