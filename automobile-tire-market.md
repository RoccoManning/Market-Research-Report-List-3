<p><p>Automobile tire market can be broadly classified into two types - Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias tires are constructed with multiple rubber plies overlapping at various angles, providing a rigid and durable structure suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Radial tires are constructed with layers of steel belts running perpendicular to the tread direction, offering better stability, fuel efficiency, and performance on different road conditions. Both market segments cater to specific needs and preferences of customers, influencing their choice in purchasing tires for their vehicles.</p></p>
