<p><p>An articulated boom crane is a heavy-duty construction equipment that consists of a crane mounted on a truck or trailer with a telescopic boom and multiple articulating sections. This configuration allows the crane to maneuver into tight spaces and reach high or difficult-to-access areas with ease. It is widely used in construction, industrial, and infrastructure projects for tasks such as lifting heavy objects, installing building materials, and placing equipment.</p><p>The articulated boom crane market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period. One of the major factors driving market growth is the increasing demand for efficient and versatile lifting equipment in various industries. As more cities and towns are expanding, the need for construction equipment, including articulated boom cranes, is increasing. For instance, the integration of technologies like telematics, remote monitoring, and automation allows operators to monitor and control the crane's performance remotely, improving efficiency and reducing the risk of accidents.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing preference for eco-friendly equipment has led to the development of electric and hybrid-powered articulated boom cranes. These cranes reduce emissions and noise levels, making them more environmentally friendly and compliant with stringent regulations.</p><p>In conclusion, the articulated boom crane market is witnessing robust growth due to the increasing demand for versatile lifting equipment in various industries and ongoing infrastructure development projects. <p><p>The Articulated Boom Crane market consists of several market types based on the number of axes. Three Axis cranes have the boom, knuckle, and arm movements controlled by three separate hydraulic cylinders. They are versatile and easy to operate. Four Axis cranes have an additional axis, enabling precise control over the boom angle. They are suitable for heavy lifting tasks and offer enhanced stability. Apart from these types, the market also includes cranes with more axes, advanced hydraulic systems, and features like telescopic booms, extending their functionality and applications.</p></p>
