<p><p>Sunflower oil is categorized into three different types based on their fatty acid composition: linoleic oil, mid-oleic oil, and high-oleic oil. Linoleic oil has a high content of polyunsaturated fatty acids, making it suitable for general cooking purposes. Mid-oleic oil has a balanced composition of monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fatty acids, making it ideal for frying and baking. High-oleic oil contains a high amount of monounsaturated fatty acids, making it more stable and long-lasting for deep frying and commercial food manufacturing. These market types cater to different preferences and cooking needs of consumers.</p></p>
