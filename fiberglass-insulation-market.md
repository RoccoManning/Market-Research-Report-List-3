<p><p>Fiberglass insulation market is segmented into three types: Continuous Fiber, Fixed Length Fiber, and Glass Wool. Continuous fiber insulation is made of long strands of fiberglass, providing better coverage and thermal resistance. Fixed length fiber insulation consists of short fibers pressed into batts or rolls, offering lower cost but still effective insulation. Glass wool insulation is made of fine glass fibers spun into a wool-like material, offering good thermal performance and sound absorption properties. Each type serves specific needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
