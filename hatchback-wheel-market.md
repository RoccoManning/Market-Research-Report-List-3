<p><p>Hatchback wheel markets can be divided into two main types: casting and forging. Casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold to create the wheel, resulting in a lower cost but potentially weaker structure. Forging, on the other hand, involves shaping heated metal with pressure to create a stronger and more durable wheel, albeit at a higher cost. Both types have their own benefits and drawbacks, and consumers can choose based on their priorities of cost and quality.</p></p>
