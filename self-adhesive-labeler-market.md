<p><p>The Self-Adhesive Labeler market can be categorized into two main types: Automatic Self-Adhesive Labelers and Semi-Automatic Self-Adhesive Labelers. Automatic labelers are fully automated machines that can apply self-adhesive labels to products without any manual intervention. They are capable of high-speed labeling and are ideal for large-scale production lines. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic labelers require some manual assistance, such as placing the product on the labeling station, but the application of labels is done automatically. They are suitable for smaller production volumes and offer a more cost-effective solution.</p></p>
