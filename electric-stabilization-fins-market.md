<p><p>Electric stabilization fins are used in various types, with two main categories being flat fins and vector fins. Flat fins are fixed devices that provide stability by increasing surface area in the water. They are typically used in smaller watercraft. On the other hand, vector fins are movable and adjustable, allowing for better maneuverability and control in larger vessels. Both types are designed to improve stability and control in rough waters, making them essential components in marine navigation and safety.</p></p>
