<p><p>The Aircraft Attitude Indicator market is divided into two types: Analog and Digital. In the Analog market, the indicators utilize mechanical gyros to provide pilots with visual reference of the aircraft's pitch and bank angles. These indicators are typically more affordable but require regular calibration and maintenance. On the other hand, the Digital market employs electronic sensors to measure and display the aircraft's attitude. These indicators are more accurate, reliable, and easier to read, but tend to be more expensive.</p></p>
