<p><p>Piezoceramic components for automotives are used in various applications to convert electrical energy into mechanical vibrations or vice versa. The market for these components can be categorized into two types: single plate piezo ceramics and multilayer piezo ceramics. </p><p>Single plate piezo ceramics consist of a single layer of piezoelectric material, which can generate a small amount of displacement. These components are commonly used in applications such as sensors and actuators.</p><p>On the other hand, multilayer piezo ceramics are composed of multiple layers of piezoelectric material stacked together, allowing for a larger displacement. These components are usually employed in more demanding applications, including fuel injectors, vibration sensors, and piezo motors.</p><p>The market for both types of piezoceramic components in the automotive industry is driven by the need for improved performance, efficiency, and reliability in automotive systems and components.</p></p>
