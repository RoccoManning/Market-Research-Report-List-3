<p><p>The market for belt alignment tools consists of two main types, namely the laser-emitting unit and the receiver unit. The laser-emitting unit is a device that projects a laser beam to accurately align belts on machinery and equipment. It provides a visual reference for alignment. On the other hand, the receiver unit is a device that detects the laser beam emitted by the laser-emitting unit. It helps ensure precise alignment of belts by accurately measuring the position of the laser beam and indicating any misalignment.</p></p>
