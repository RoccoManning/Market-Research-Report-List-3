<p><ul><li>Strut Landing Gear</li><li>Rocker Landing Gear</li><li>Pontoon Landing Gear</li><li>Framed Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft landing gear systems come in various types to cater to different aircraft needs. Strut landing gear is a widely used system that includes a hydraulic strut for shock absorption. Rocker landing gear uses a pivoting motion to absorb landing impact. Pontoon landing gear is designed for floatplanes and seaplanes to support landings on water. Framed landing gear consists of a rigid framework for added stability during touchdown. Each market type serves specific aircraft requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
