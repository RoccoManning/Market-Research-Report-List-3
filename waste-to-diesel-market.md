Gasification involves converting organic materials into syngas, which can then be converted into diesel. Pyrolysis uses high temperatures to break down waste into oil, gas, and char which can be refined into diesel. Depolymerisation breaks down plastics into smaller molecules for diesel production. Incineration burns waste to generate heat for diesel production.</p></p>
