<p><strong>The Desktop Turbidity Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scattered Light Turbidimeter</li><li>Transmitted Light Turbidimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desktop turbidity meters market consists of two main types: scattered light turbidimeters and transmitted light turbidimeters. Scattered light turbidimeters operate by measuring the amount of light scattered by suspended particles in a liquid sample, providing a reading of the turbidity level. On the other hand, transmitted light turbidimeters work by measuring the reduction in light intensity as it passes through a liquid sample, indicating the turbidity level. Both types of turbidimeters are used to determine the clarity or cloudiness of a liquid and find applications in various industries.</p></p>
