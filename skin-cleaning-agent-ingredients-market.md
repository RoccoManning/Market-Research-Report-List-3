<p><p>Skin cleaning agent ingredients market can be categorized into three types based on their chemical properties: anionic cleaners, non-ionic cleaners, and amphoteric cleaners. Anionic cleaners are negatively charged and effective in removing dirt and oil from the skin. Non-ionic cleaners are neutral and gentle, making them suitable for sensitive skin. Amphoteric cleaners have both positive and negative charges, providing versatility in their cleaning abilities. Each type of cleaner offers unique benefits and is used in various skincare products.</p></p>
