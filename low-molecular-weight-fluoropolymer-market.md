<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Synthesis</li><li>Telomerization</li><li>Oligomerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Molecular Weight Fluoropolymer market types include Electrochemical Synthesis, Telomerization, and Oligomerization. In Electrochemical Synthesis, fluoropolymers are produced through electrochemical reactions, offering a precise control over the polymer structure. Telomerization involves the addition of telogens to form fluoropolymers with specific chain lengths. Oligomerization creates low molecular weight fluoropolymers by combining small fluoropolymer molecules. Each method has its own advantages in terms of efficiency, control over molecular weight, and customization of fluoropolymer properties for various industrial applications.</p></p>
