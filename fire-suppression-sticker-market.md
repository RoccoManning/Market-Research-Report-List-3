<p><p>The fire suppression sticker market consists of two main types: inert gasses and aerogel. Inert gasses type fire suppression stickers are designed to release a mixture of non-reactive gases when exposed to heat, effectively displacing oxygen and suppressing the fire. On the other hand, aerogel type fire suppression stickers contain a highly porous material that absorbs heat and restricts oxygen supply to the fire, preventing its spread. Both types aim to provide efficient and safe fire suppression solutions for various applications.</p></p>
