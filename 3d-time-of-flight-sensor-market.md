<p><p>The 3D Time-of-Flight (TOF) Sensor market can be categorized into three types based on measurement techniques. Continuous Wave Measurement utilizes continuous wave signals to measure the time taken for light to travel from the sensor to an object and back. Pulse Measurement involves emitting short pulses of light and measuring the time it takes for the pulses to return. Other types include hybrid techniques combining continuous wave and pulse measurements. These different market types cater to diverse applications and offer varying levels of accuracy and functionalities for 3D TOF sensing.</p></p>
