<p><p>In the marine market, various types of air compressors are used for different purposes. The main air compressor is the primary source of compressed air on a ship, powering various systems and equipment. Topping up compressors are smaller units used for topping up the air supply in the main compressor. Deck air compressors are specifically designed for use on the ship's deck, providing compressed air for deck machinery and operations. The "Others" market includes specialized air compressors for specific applications, such as emergency air supply or diving operations.</p></p>
