<p><p>Sound Insulation Windows are designed to reduce external noise entering a building, creating a more peaceful interior environment. There are two main types of Sound Insulation Windows on the market: Laminated Glass windows and Vacuum Glass windows. Laminated Glass windows consist of two or more layers of glass with a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) sandwiched in between, providing sound insulation by absorbing vibrations. Vacuum Glass windows contain a vacuum-sealed space between two panes of glass, preventing sound transmission through the window.</p></p>
