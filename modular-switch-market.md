<p><p>The modular switch market comprises various types of switches including toggle switches, rocker switches, and plate switches. Toggle switches are switches that can be flipped up or down to turn on or off the connected device. Rocker switches have a pivoting actuator that rocks back and forth to control the flow of electricity. Plate switches are flat switches that are usually installed on a wall or a panel. All of these switches are modular in nature, meaning they can be easily replaced or interchanged with other switches in a modular switch system.</p></p>
