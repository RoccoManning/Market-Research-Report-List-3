<p><ul><li>Through-Beam</li><li>Retro-Reflective</li><li>Diffuse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT Smart Ultrasonic Proximity Sensors market consists of three main types: Through-Beam, Retro-Reflective, and Diffuse. Through-Beam sensors use a transmitter and receiver placed opposite each other to detect objects passing between them. Retro-Reflective sensors use a reflector to bounce ultrasonic waves back to the sensor. Diffuse sensors emit ultrasonic waves and detect reflected waves from objects within their range. Each type has its unique advantages and applications in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
