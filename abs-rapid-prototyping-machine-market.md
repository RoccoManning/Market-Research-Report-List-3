<p><p>The ABS Rapid Prototyping Machine market consists of two major types: FDM Technology and MEM Technology. FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) Technology employs a nozzle to melt and deposit a thermoplastic material layer by layer, allowing for the creation of detailed prototypes quickly and cost-effectively. On the other hand, MEM (Micro Electro-Mechanical) Technology employs intricate microfabrication techniques to create small, complex prototypes. Both technologies are widely used in industries requiring rapid and precise prototyping, offering different advantages and applications.</p></p>
