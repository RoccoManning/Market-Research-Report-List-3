<p><strong>The Infrared Cutting Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Type</li><li>Reflection Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared cutting glass market includes two primary types: absorption type and reflection type. Absorption type cutting uses the energy from infrared light to heat and melt the glass, allowing for precise cutting. On the other hand, reflection type cutting involves using infrared light to create a high-pressure gas that blows away the melted glass, achieving clean cuts. Both methods are utilized in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and architecture for cutting glass with precision and efficiency.</p></p>
