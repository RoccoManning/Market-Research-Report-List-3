<p><p>The solar inverter market consists of various types. Central solar inverters are commonly used in large-scale solar power plants, as they have higher power outputs and are designed for maximum efficiency. String solar inverters are used in medium-sized solar installations, where a series of solar panels are connected to the inverter using strings. Micro solar inverters, on the other hand, are designed for residential or small-scale applications, as they are installed on each individual solar panel to optimize energy production and provide system monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
