<p><p>Flexible press market types refer to the different printing capabilities of flexographic presses. 2-color flexographic presses can print using two different ink colors on a substrate, offering basic design flexibility. 4-color presses, also known as CMYK presses, utilize cyan, magenta, yellow, and black inks to produce a wider range of colors and detailed images. 6-color presses enhance print quality further by including additional spot color inks. The above market category encompasses presses with more than six colors, providing even greater color accuracy and versatility for complex designs, packaging, or high-quality printing requirements.</p></p>
