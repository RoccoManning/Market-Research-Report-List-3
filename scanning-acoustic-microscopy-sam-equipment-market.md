<p><p>Scanning acoustic microscopy (SAM) equipment is a non-destructive testing technique that utilizes ultrasound waves to obtain high-resolution images of internal structures and material properties. The primary driving factor for this growth is the increasing demand for non-destructive testing methods for quality control and failure analysis in various industries. The SAM equipment offers advantages over other testing techniques like x-ray, optical microscopy, and electron microscopy as it provides detailed information about the internal structure without damaging the sample.</p><p>Moreover, the rising complexity and miniaturization of electronic devices and components, along with the growing demand for high-quality products, are expected to boost the adoption of SAM equipment.
