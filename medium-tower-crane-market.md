<p><strong>The Medium Tower Crane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-erecting Tower Cranes</li><li>Flat Top Tower Cranes</li><li>Hammerhead Tower Cranes</li><li>Luffing Jib Tower Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium tower crane market consists of various types of cranes used in construction and other industries. Self-erecting tower cranes are easily transportable and can be assembled without external help. Flat top tower cranes have a horizontal jib without a trolley, allowing for higher lifting capacities. Hammerhead tower cranes have a distinctive shape, with a horizontal jib and a counterweight resembling the head of a hammer. Luffing jib tower cranes have a variable-angle jib that can be adjusted to overcome obstacles and work in confined spaces. These different types cater to specific construction needs and project requirements.</p></p>
