<p><p>Ceilometers are instruments used to measure the height of the base of clouds above the ground. Laser-drum Ceilometers use lasers to determine cloud height, while Optical Ceilometers use optical sensors. Laser-drum Ceilometers are typically more accurate and reliable, making them a popular choice for meteorological applications. Optical Ceilometers are simpler and more cost-effective, making them suitable for less critical or research purposes. Both types of Ceilometers play a crucial role in weather forecasting and aviation safety.</p></p>
