<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Optical Magnetometers are devices used to measure the strength and direction of magnetic fields. Absolute Magnetometers provide an accurate measurement of the absolute strength of a magnetic field, while Relative Magnetometers measure changes in magnetic field strength relative to a reference point. Both types of magnetometers have applications in various industries such as geophysics, navigation, and aerospace. Absolute Magnetometers are used when precise measurements are required, while Relative Magnetometers are more commonly used for detecting changes in magnetic fields.</p></p>
