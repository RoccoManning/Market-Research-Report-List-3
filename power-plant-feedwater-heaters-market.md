<p><strong>The Power Plant Feedwater Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-pressure Power Plant Feedwater Heaters</li><li>High-pressure Power Plant Feedwater Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Plant Feedwater Heaters are devices used to heat the water that is fed into a power plant's boiler before it is converted into steam. There are two main types of Power Plant Feedwater Heaters in the market: Low-pressure and High-pressure. Low-pressure heaters heat the water using low-pressure steam and are generally located after the condenser. High-pressure heaters, on the other hand, utilize high-pressure steam from the turbine to heat the feedwater and are typically placed before the boiler. Both types help improve the overall efficiency of power generation by recovering heat energy from the plant's operations.</p></p>
