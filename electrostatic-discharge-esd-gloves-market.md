<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) gloves are specialized gloves designed to prevent the buildup and discharge of static electricity in order to protect sensitive electronic components from damage. These gloves are commonly used in industries such as electronics manufacturing, telecommunications, aerospace, and healthcare where electrostatic discharge can cause significant harm to equipment and products.</p><p>ESD gloves are made of materials with low surface resistance, such as carbon fiber or conductive fibers, and are designed to dissipate electrostatic charges without generating additional static electricity. By wearing ESD gloves, workers can handle electronic components safely without the risk of damaging them through electrostatic discharge.</p><p>The current outlook for the ESD gloves market is positive and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.9% during the forecasted period.
