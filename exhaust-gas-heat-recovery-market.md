<p><p>Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) market includes two main types: exchanger and boiler market. In the exchanger market, heat is recovered from exhaust gas and transferred to a medium, such as water or air, through a heat exchanger. This recovered heat can be used for various applications, such as space heating or hot water production. On the other hand, the boiler market involves the recovery of heat from exhaust gas to generate steam or hot water, which can be used for industrial processes or power generation. Both markets aim to utilize waste heat from exhaust gases for energy efficiency and sustainability purposes.</p></p>
