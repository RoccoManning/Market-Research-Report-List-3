<p><p>A Faraday optical isolator is an optical device used to prevent the back reflection of light in a fiber optic system. It is based on the Faraday effect, which is the rotation of the polarization plane of light in the presence of a magnetic field. The isolator allows light to pass through it in one direction while blocking any back reflection or light that tries to travel in the opposite direction.</p><p>The current outlook of the Faraday optical isolator market is quite positive. The increasing demand for high-speed and high-performance optical networks is one of the major factors driving market growth.
