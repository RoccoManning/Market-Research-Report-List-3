<p><p>The air cooled screw chiller market offers two types of adjustment methods: electrodeless adjustment and sectional adjustment. Electrodeless adjustment refers to the ability to adjust the cooling capacity of the chiller without the need for any physical contact or electrodes. This method allows for precise and efficient control of cooling capacity. This provides flexibility in matching the cooling requirements of different applications and allows for energy optimization.</p></p>
