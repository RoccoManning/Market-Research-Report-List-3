<p><p>PTFE Micropowder market types include Monomer Polymerization and Resin Degradation. Monomer Polymerization involves the conversion of PTFE monomers into micropowder form through a polymerization process. On the other hand, Resin Degradation market type involves breaking down PTFE resin into micropowder particles. Both processes contribute to the production and availability of PTFE micropowder, which is used in various industries such as coatings, lubricants, and textiles for its unique properties like chemical resistance and low friction.</p></p>
