<p><p>An oil immersion objective lens is a type of microscope objective lens that requires the immersion of both the specimen being observed and the objective lens itself in a transparent oil with a high refractive index. This oil helps to improve the resolution and clarity of the image by minimizing the refraction and scattering of light. Oil immersion objective lenses are commonly used in high-resolution microscopy techniques such as confocal microscopy and fluorescence microscopy, where a high level of detail and clarity is essential.</p><p>The oil immersion objective lens market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The oil immersion objective lens market can be categorized into different types: Type N, Type B, Type F, and Others. Type B lenses are suitable for industrial applications as they offer a long working distance and large field of view.
