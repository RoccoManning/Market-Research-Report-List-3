<p><p>The Offline Voice Module market consists of two main types: the Offline Speech Recognition Module and the Offline Speech Synthesis Module. The Offline Speech Recognition Module enables devices to recognize and convert spoken languages into written text without an internet connection. In contrast, the Offline Speech Synthesis Module allows devices to convert written text into spoken language offline. Both types of modules play a crucial role in enhancing the functionality and usability of devices for tasks such as transcription, voice commands, and natural language processing without relying on an internet connection.</p></p>
