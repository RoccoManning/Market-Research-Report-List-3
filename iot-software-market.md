<p><p>In the IoT software market, there are two main types: Cloud-based and On-premises solutions. Cloud-based IoT software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering scalability and flexibility. On the other hand, On-premises IoT software is installed and operated on the company's own servers or data centers, providing greater control over data security and compliance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of businesses depending on factors like data sensitivity, cost, and ease of deployment.</p></p>
