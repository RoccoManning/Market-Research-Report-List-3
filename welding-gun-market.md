<p><p>The welding gun market consists of two main types: arc welding and resistance welding. </p><p>Arc welding involves using an electric arc to create heat, which melts and joins metals together. This type of welding is commonly used in construction, automotive, and shipbuilding industries. </p><p>Resistance welding, on the other hand, uses electrical resistance to generate heat and join metals. It is widely used in the manufacturing of appliances, automotive parts, and electrical equipment. Both arc welding and resistance welding play a crucial role in various industries due to their effectiveness in joining different kinds of metals.</p></p>
