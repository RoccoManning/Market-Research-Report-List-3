<p><p>Industrial vacuum thermoforming machines are classified into three types based on operation mode: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic machines require minimal human intervention and can carry out the entire thermoforming process independently. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input, such as loading and unloading materials. Manual machines rely entirely on human operation for each step of the thermoforming process. Each type offers different levels of efficiency, productivity, and cost-effectiveness, catering to a wide range of industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
