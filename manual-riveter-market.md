<p><strong>The Manual Riveter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual riveter market types can be categorized into automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic riveters, as the name suggests, require minimal human intervention as they can automatically feed and set the rivets. They are ideal for high-volume production, offering efficiency and speed. On the other hand, semi-automatic riveters require some manual assistance, such as feeding the rivets, while the rivet setting process is automated. These riveters strike a balance between manual and automated functions, catering to medium-volume production needs. Both types offer versatility and are widely used in industries requiring efficient and precise riveting.</p></p>
