<p><strong>The IV Solutions in Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic Solutions</li><li>Hypotonic Solutions</li><li>Hypertonic Solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IV solutions in containers are classified into three main types based on their solute concentration: isotonic solutions, hypotonic solutions, and hypertonic solutions. Isotonic solutions have the same concentration of solutes as human blood and are used to replenish fluids and electrolytes. Hypotonic solutions have a lower concentration of solutes and are used to hydrate cells. Hypertonic solutions have a higher concentration of solutes and are used to treat dehydration and increase blood pressure. The market for these different types of IV solutions is driven by various medical conditions and treatment needs.</p></p>
