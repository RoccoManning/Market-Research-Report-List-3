<p><p>Mechanical fatigue testing systems are available in two main types: semi-automatic and full-automatic. Semi-automatic systems require some level of manual input from the operator, such as setting up the test parameters and initiating the test process. Full-automatic systems, on the other hand, are more advanced and require minimal to no manual input once the test parameters are set. They are capable of running multiple tests automatically without the need for continuous monitoring from an operator. Both types are widely used in various industries for testing the durability and reliability of materials and products.</p></p>
