<p><p>Both Freeze Recovery and Wheel Recovery are types of NMP recycling systems used to recover and recycle N-methyl-2-pyrrolidone (NMP) solvent in industrial processes. Freeze Recovery involves freezing the NMP solvent and then separating it from impurities, while Wheel Recovery uses a rotating wheel to extract the NMP solvent from the waste. These systems are important in reducing waste and ensuring efficient use of resources in industries that use NMP solvent.</p></p>
