<p><strong>The IoT RFID Electronic Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive RFID electronic tags do not have their power source and rely on the energy emitted by the RFID reader to transmit data. They are cost-effective and commonly used for tracking assets in supply chain management. Active RFID electronic tags have their power source and transmit data continuously, allowing for real-time tracking of assets. They are more expensive but offer longer read ranges and can be used for monitoring high-value assets. Both types play a crucial role in the IoT RFID electronic tags market for diverse applications.</p></p>
