<p><p>MOSFET RF transistors are commonly available in two main types - dual N-channel and N-channel transistors. Dual N-channel transistors consist of two parallel N-channel devices within a single package, allowing for enhanced performance and efficiency in high-frequency applications. On the other hand, N-channel transistors are single-channel devices that offer a simpler design but are still effective for RF amplification and switching purposes. Both types cater to the varying needs of RF circuit designers in terms of functionality and performance.</p></p>
