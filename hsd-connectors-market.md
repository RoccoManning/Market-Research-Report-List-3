<p><p>HSD Connectors market includes three main types: wire to wire, wire to board, and board to board connectors. Wire to wire connectors are used to join two separate wires, ensuring seamless transmission of electrical signals. Wire to board connectors connect wires to a circuit board, providing a stable electrical connection. Lastly, board to board connectors connect two circuit boards, allowing data and power transfer between them. These connectors are essential for various electronic devices and applications where reliable and efficient connections are required.</p></p>
