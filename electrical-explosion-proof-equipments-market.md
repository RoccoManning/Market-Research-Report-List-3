<p><p>The electrical explosion-proof equipment market consists of various types designed to prevent explosions in hazardous environments. Flame-proof type equipment contains any potential sparks or flames within a robust enclosure. Increased safety type equipment reduces the risk of ignition by restricting energy and temperature levels. Intrinsic safety type equipment uses low energy levels to prevent sparks. Positive-pressure type equipment keeps explosive gases out. Oil-immersed type equipment uses oil or a dielectric liquid for insulation. Sand-filled type equipment uses an inert material to prevent explosions. Other types include various specialized solutions for specific environments.</p></p>
