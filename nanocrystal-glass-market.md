<p><p>Nanocrystal glass can be categorized into two main types: Single Crystal and Multi Crystal. Single Crystal nanocrystal glass is made up of one continuous crystal structure, which results in superior optical clarity and mechanical strength. On the other hand, Multi Crystal nanocrystal glass consists of multiple crystal structures, leading to improved flexibility and easier manufacturing. Both types have their unique advantages and are widely used in various industries such as electronics, healthcare, and construction for their exceptional properties.</p></p>
