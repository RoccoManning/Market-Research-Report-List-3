Three-way solenoid valves have one inlet and two outlets, providing options for diverting or mixing fluids. Four-way solenoid valves have two inlets and two outlets, enabling the control of flow direction or allowing for complex fluid routing. These valves are widely used in various industries to control fluid flow in a controlled and efficient manner.</p></p>
