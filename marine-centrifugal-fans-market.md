<p><strong>The Marine Centrifugal Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Belt Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine centrifugal fans are commonly divided into two types based on their drive mechanisms: direct drive and belt drive. Direct drive fans are directly connected to the motor, resulting in higher efficiency and less maintenance. On the other hand, belt drive fans use belts and pulleys to connect the motor to the fan, allowing for more flexibility in speed and airflow adjustment. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications in the marine industry.</p></p>
