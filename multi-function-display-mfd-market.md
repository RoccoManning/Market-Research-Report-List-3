<p><p>The Multi Function Display (MFD) market consists of different types of displays, including LED, Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), LCD monitor, Active Matrix Liquid Crystal Display (AMLCD), and Thin Film Transistor (TFT) market. LED displays utilize light-emitting diodes to produce bright and energy-efficient visuals. OLED displays feature organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is passed through them. LCD monitors use liquid crystal technology to display images and video. AMLCDs utilize a matrix of thin-film transistors to enhance image quality and response time. TFT displays are thin and use transistors that control individual pixels, providing high-quality visuals.</p></p>
