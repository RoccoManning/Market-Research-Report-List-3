<p><strong>The Anti Submarine Rocket Launching System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Launch</li><li>Cold Launch</li><li>Concentric Canister Launch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-submarine rocket launching system market consists of three types of launch methods: hot launch, cold launch, and concentric canister launch. Hot launch involves launching the rocket from the submarine's torpedo tube while the submarine is still submerged. Cold launch involves ejecting the rocket from the submarine's torpedo tube using gas pressure once the submarine has surfaced. Concentric canister launch is a method where the rocket is launched from a canister on the surface of the water, separate from the submarine. Each launch method has its advantages and is utilized based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
