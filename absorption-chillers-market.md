<p><p>Absorption chillers are categorized into Single Stage and Double Stage markets based on the number of steps involved in the refrigeration process. In Single Stage absorption chillers, cooling is achieved in one step, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Double Stage absorption chillers involve two steps, which increases efficiency and cooling capacity but also makes them more complex and expensive. Both types offer distinct advantages and are chosen based on specific cooling requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
