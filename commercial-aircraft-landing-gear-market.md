<p><ul><li>Strut Landing Gear</li><li>Rocker Landing Gear</li><li>Pontoon Landing Gear</li><li>Framed Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aircraft landing gear market encompasses different types of landing gear systems. Strut landing gear refers to a system where the landing gear legs are attached to the aircraft using a single strut. Rocker landing gear features landing gear legs that pivot on a rocking mechanism, allowing the gear to retract. Pontoon landing gear uses floats or pontoons for landing on water surfaces. Framed landing gear includes a combination of multiple struts and braces to support the aircraft during landing and takeoff. These variations cater to different aircraft needs and operational environments.</p></p>
