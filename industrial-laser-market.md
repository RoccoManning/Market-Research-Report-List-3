<p><p>The industrial laser market can be divided into several types, namely CO2 lasers, fiber lasers, solid-state lasers, and others. CO2 lasers use carbon dioxide gas as the active medium to produce a continuous laser beam, and they are commonly employed for cutting, welding, and marking applications. Fiber lasers utilize an optical fiber as the gain medium, offering high output power and excellent beam quality, making them suitable for various industrial processes. Solid-state lasers employ a solid material (such as crystal or glass) as the active medium and are known for their high-energy pulses. Lastly, the "others" category includes various types of lasers like diode-pumped lasers, excimer lasers, and semiconductor lasers used for specific industrial purposes.</p></p>
