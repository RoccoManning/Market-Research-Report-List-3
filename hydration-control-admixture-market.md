<p><p>Hydration control admixtures are essential additives used in concrete to regulate the setting time and workability of the mixture. These admixtures are available in two main forms: liquid and powder. Liquid admixtures are pre-dissolved solutions added directly to the concrete mix, while powder admixtures are usually added as a dry powder to the mix. Both types of admixtures work by controlling the hydration process of the cement, resulting in a more durable and workable concrete mixture.</p></p>
