<p><strong>The Ultraviolet-Curing Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiation Curing</li><li>Cation Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultraviolet-Curing Resin Market includes two main types: Radiation Curing and Cation Curing. Radiation Curing involves using ultraviolet light to initiate a chemical reaction that hardens the resin. This method is popular in industries like printing, electronics, and automotive coatings. On the other hand, Cation Curing involves the use of a cationic photoinitiator to initiate the curing process. This method is commonly used in industries like adhesives, sealants, and dental materials. Both types of curing offer fast and efficient curing processes for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
