<p><p>The Battery-Powered Nail Gun market can be classified into two types: Wireless Battery-Powered Nail Gun and Cable Battery-Powered Nail Gun. On the other hand, Cable Battery-Powered Nail Guns require a power cable to connect to an external power source. While they may offer a more consistent power supply, they are less portable compared to their wireless counterparts.</p></p>
