<p><p>The adjustable linear regulators market consists of two main types: single-channel and multi-channel regulators. A single-channel regulator allows for the adjustment of only one output voltage, making it suitable for applications where a single voltage level is required. On the other hand, a multi-channel regulator enables the adjustment of multiple output voltages independently, making it ideal for applications that require different voltage levels for different components or subsystems. Both types of regulators play a significant role in providing stable and adjustable voltage levels, ensuring efficient operation of electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
