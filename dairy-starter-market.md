<p><p>The dairy starter market consists of different types based on the microorganisms used in the starter cultures. Mesophilic starters are made with bacteria that thrive at moderate temperatures, commonly used in making soft cheeses and cultured milk products. Thermophilic starters contain bacteria that can withstand high temperatures, suitable for making yogurt and hard cheeses. Probiotic starters are designed to promote gut health, containing live bacteria beneficial for digestion and immunity. These different types cater to various dairy products, each offering unique characteristics and benefits for the final product.</p></p>
