<p><p>Construction tractors are heavy-duty vehicles used in construction projects. Crawler tractors are equipped with continuous tracks, offering excellent traction and maneuverability on rough terrains, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. They are commonly used in mining and large-scale construction projects. On the other hand, wheeled tractors, as the name suggests, have wheels instead of tracks. They provide faster speeds and greater mobility, making them ideal for general construction tasks, such as grading and leveling. Wheeled tractors are commonly used in urban construction sites where speed and flexibility are vital.</p></p>
