<p><p>The strong magnetic separator market is characterized by two types: dry and wet separators. Dry strong magnetic separators are designed for processing materials with dry magnetic properties, such as hematite, magnetite, and ilmenite. They are widely used in the recycling industry to remove ferromagnetic impurities. Wet strong magnetic separators, on the other hand, are designed for materials with wet magnetic properties. They are commonly used in the mineral processing industry to separate magnetic minerals from non-magnetic minerals, improving the purity and quality of the final product.</p></p>
