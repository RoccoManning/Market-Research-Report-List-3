<p><p>Production freeze dryers are specialized machines used for preserving perishable items such as food and pharmaceuticals by removing moisture through the process of sublimation. There are two main types of production freeze dryers: table top and freestanding. Table top freeze dryers are compact and portable, suitable for limited space environments. Freestanding freeze dryers are larger and offer higher capacity, making them ideal for bigger productions. Both types possess similar functionalities and use vacuum technology to extract moisture, but vary in size and capacity to suit different production needs.</p></p>
