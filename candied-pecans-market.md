<p><p>Candied pecans market consists of two main types: in-shell pecans and shelled pecans. In-shell pecans refer to pecans that are still enclosed in their hard outer shell, requiring consumers to crack them open before consumption. These are favored by some individuals as they believe the shell protects the nut from moisture and provides a fresher taste. On the other hand, shelled pecans are already removed from their shells, offering convenience to consumers who prefer ready-to-eat pecans. These two market types cater to different preferences in terms of taste, texture, and ease of consumption.</p></p>
