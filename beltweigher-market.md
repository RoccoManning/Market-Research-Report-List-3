<p><p>The Beltweigher market offers two main types of products: Single-idler Beltweigher and Multi-idler Beltweigher. A Single-idler Beltweigher consists of a single load cell mounted on a frame and used for measuring the weight of bulk materials on conveyor belts. On the other hand, a Multi-idler Beltweigher uses multiple load cells evenly distributed across the frame for improved accuracy and stability. These beltweighers are commonly used in industries such as mining, food processing, and logistics to accurately track the weight of materials being transported on conveyor belts.</p></p>
