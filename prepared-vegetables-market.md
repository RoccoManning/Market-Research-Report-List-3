<p><p>The Prepared Vegetables market consists of three main types: Ready to Eat (RTE), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Cook (RTC). RTE vegetables are washed, cut, and packaged, requiring no additional preparation before consumption. RTH vegetables are partially cooked and require heating before eating. RTC vegetables are raw or minimally processed, requiring cooking before consumption. These market types cater to different consumer needs, offering convenience and time-saving options for those who prefer fully prepared meals, quick heating options, or prefer to cook their vegetables from scratch.</p></p>
