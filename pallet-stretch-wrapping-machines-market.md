<p><p>The Pallet Stretch Wrapping Machines market consists of three types: Semi-Automatic, Automatic, and Manual. Semi-Automatic machines require some manual intervention in the wrapping process, such as attaching the film to the pallet and cutting it manually. Automatic machines, on the other hand, perform the entire wrapping process automatically, from attaching the film to cutting it. Manual machines are operated solely by human labor, where each step of the wrapping process is done manually. These three types cater to different levels of automation and labor requirements in the market.</p></p>
