Fully automatic machines operate independently, from tube loading to sealing, with minimal human involvement. Manual machines, on the other hand, require complete human control for each step of the sealing process. Each market type offers different levels of automation, with fully automatic machines being the most efficient and manual machines requiring more manual labor.</p></p>
