<p><p>The DTH Hammer market is categorized into different types based on the shape of the face of the hammer. Convex-faced DTH Hammers have a slightly rounded impact surface, which helps in reducing stress and wear on the drill bit. Flat-faced DTH Hammers have a flat impact surface, providing higher impact energy and stability during drilling. Concave-faced DTH Hammers feature a curved impact surface that is useful for drilling at larger angles. Apart from these main types, there are also other variations available in the market, each offering specific advantages for different drilling applications.</p></p>
