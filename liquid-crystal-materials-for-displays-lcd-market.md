STN (Super Twisted Nematic) type Liquid Crystal Materials offer better contrast and wider viewing angles for automotive and industrial applications. HTN (High-Temperature Nematic) type Liquid Crystal Materials are specifically designed for high-temperature environments. These materials cater to different display needs and find applications in a range of industries.</p></p>
