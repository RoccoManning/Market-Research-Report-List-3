<p><p>Deck bushings are components used in various industrial applications such as machinery and equipment. They come in two main market types: static and rotating. Static deck bushings are designed to remain fixed in position and provide stability and support. They are commonly used in applications where there is no need for rotational movement. On the other hand, rotating deck bushings are specifically designed to allow rotational movement, providing a smooth and efficient operation in applications where rotation is required. Both market types cater to different functional requirements in the industry.</p></p>
