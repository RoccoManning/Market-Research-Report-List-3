<p><p>Dental vacuum pumps are essential equipment used in dental clinics to remove fluids, debris, and other waste materials generated during dental procedures. Two types of dental vacuum pumps are commonly used: wet vacuum pumps and dry vacuum pumps. Wet vacuum pumps use water as a sealant and are designed for high-volume suction, making them ideal for procedures like oral surgery. In contrast, dry vacuum pumps do not use water and rely on air and mechanical components for suction, making them suitable for general dental procedures. Both types play a crucial role in dental clinics' efficient operation.</p></p>
