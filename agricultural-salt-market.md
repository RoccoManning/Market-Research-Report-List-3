<p><p>Agricultural salt market consists of three main types: sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is produced through the evaporation of seawater, providing a high mineral content. Well and rock salt are mined from underground deposits and have a more concentrated sodium chloride content. Lake salt is extracted from salt water lakes and is known for its unique flavor profile. Each type of salt offers distinct characteristics and benefits for agricultural use.</p></p>
