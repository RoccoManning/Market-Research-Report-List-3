<p><p>Flexible paper food and beverage packaging can be categorized into different types based on the materials used. PET packaging is made from polyethylene terephthalate, which is lightweight and transparent, making it ideal for products like juices and snacks. PE packaging, made from polyethylene, is flexible and durable, commonly used for items like condiments and sauces. PP packaging, constructed from polypropylene, is heat resistant and great for microwaveable meals. Other types of flexible paper packaging include biodegradable and compostable materials, offering eco-friendly options for consumers.</p></p>
