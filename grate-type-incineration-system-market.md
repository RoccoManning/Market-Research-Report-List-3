<p><p>The Grate Type Incineration System market consists of three main types: the Reciprocating Grate Stoker, Step Grate Stoker, and Roller Grate Stoker. Reciprocating Grate Stokers have moving grates that efficiently handle solid waste by pushing it towards the combustion zone. Step Grate Stokers use a series of steps to move the waste through the combustion process. Roller Grate Stokers have rotating grates that efficiently burn the waste by continuously moving it forward. These different types of grates provide various benefits and options for waste management and incineration processes.</p></p>
