<p><p>Tomato powder is available in various market types, including Spray Dried, Freeze Dried, and Others. Spray Dried tomato powder is created by spraying liquid tomato onto hot air, resulting in powder form. This process retains flavor, color, and aroma while offering a longer shelf life. Freeze Dried tomato powder involves freezing tomato puree and then drying it in low pressure, resulting in a free-flowing powder with minimal nutrient loss. Other market types may include air-dried, sun-dried, or dehydrated tomato powder, offering different flavors and textures.</p></p>
