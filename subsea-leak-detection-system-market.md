<p><strong>The Subsea Leak Detection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Subsea Leak Detection System</li><li>Passive Subsea Leak Detection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea leak detection system market can be categorized into two types: active subsea leak detection system and passive subsea leak detection system. </p><p>An active subsea leak detection system uses sensors and equipment to actively monitor the subsea environment for any signs of leaks. It employs techniques such as pressure and temperature monitoring, flow rate measurement, and acoustic sensing to detect and locate leaks in real-time. </p><p>On the other hand, a passive subsea leak detection system relies on the natural changes in the subsea environment caused by a leak. It detects leaks by monitoring changes in water chemistry, temperature, sound, or other parameters.</p><p>Both systems play a crucial role in detecting and preventing subsea leaks, ensuring the safety and integrity of subsea infrastructure.</p></p>
