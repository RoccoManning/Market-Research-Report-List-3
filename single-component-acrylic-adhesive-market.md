<p><p>Single-component acrylic adhesives are classified into three types based on their composition - water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based acrylic adhesives are environmentally-friendly, offer fast drying times, and are easy to clean up. Solvent-based acrylic adhesives provide high bonding strength and are suitable for a wide range of substrates. The "others" category includes special formulations such as UV-cured or pressure-sensitive adhesives. Each type has its own unique set of properties and applications, catering to different needs of the market.</p></p>
