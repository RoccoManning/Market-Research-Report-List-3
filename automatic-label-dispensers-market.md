<p><p>Automatic label dispensers are devices used to automatically dispense adhesive labels. These devices can be classified into two main types: semi-automatic label dispensers and fully automatic label dispensers. Semi-automatic label dispensers require manual input to separate and dispense labels, whereas fully automatic label dispensers can perform these tasks autonomously without human intervention. Semi-automatic label dispensers are suitable for low-volume labeling needs, while fully automatic label dispensers are designed for high-volume applications, providing greater efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
