<p><strong>The Incremental Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoders</li><li>Incremental Linear Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Incremental encoders are devices used to measure or monitor the rotational or linear position of an object. There are two common types: incremental rotary encoders and incremental linear encoders. Incremental rotary encoders are typically used in rotating machinery to determine the angle or speed of rotation. They produce a series of electrical pulses as the encoder shaft rotates. Incremental linear encoders, on the other hand, are used for linear motion applications, providing positional feedback in terms of linear displacement. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including manufacturing, automation, and robotics.</p></p>
