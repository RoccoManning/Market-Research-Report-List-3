<p><p>The bearing plunger pump market includes various types of pumps, such as the variable displacement axial piston pump and the quantitative axial piston pump. Variable displacement axial piston pumps are designed to adjust the displacement of the pump, allowing for precise control of the flow rate. On the other hand, quantitative axial piston pumps have a fixed displacement and are used for applications that require a constant, predetermined flow rate. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, industrial manufacturing, and construction.</p></p>
