<p><p>Automatic biochemistry analyzers are available in two main types: floor-standing and bench-top. Floor-standing analyzers are larger in size and are suitable for high-throughput clinical laboratories that require a high volume of testing. They typically have more advanced features and capabilities. On the other hand, bench-top analyzers are smaller in size and are more compact, making them ideal for smaller laboratories or point-of-care testing. They are easier to accommodate in limited spaces and are more cost-effective for smaller testing volumes.</p></p>
