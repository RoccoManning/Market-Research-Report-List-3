<p><p>The Automatic Riveting Machine Market includes various types of machines that are used for different riveting processes. Impact Riveting machines use a high-impact force to join materials together. Orbital Riveting machines rotate the rivet around the joint, ensuring a secure connection. Radial (Spiralform) Riveting machines create a spiral-shaped joint by spinning the rivet into the material. Rollerform Riveting machines use rollers to create a strong bond by compressing the rivet. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries that require automated riveting processes.</p></p>
