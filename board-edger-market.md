<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The board edger market consists of two main types: single blade and twin blade board edgers. Single blade board edgers are designed with a single cutting blade that trims the sides of rough boards, removing defects and creating straight edges. They are typically used by small to medium-sized sawmills. Twin blade board edgers, on the other hand, feature two cutting blades that can perform multiple cutting operations simultaneously, increasing processing efficiency. These are commonly used in larger sawmills and lumber processing facilities to handle higher production volumes.</p></p>
