It is mainly used in applications where localized temperature measurements are required. On the other hand, the Distributed Type Temperature Sensor market focuses on sensors that provide temperature measurements continuously along the length of the optical fiber. These sensors are commonly used in applications where temperature profiles or gradients need to be monitored, such as in industrial processes or environmental monitoring.</p></p>
