<p><p>The Portable X-Ray Equipment for Security Purposes market is divided into two types: CR Equipment and DR Equipment. CR Equipment refers to Computed Radiography equipment that uses cassettes to capture X-ray images. These cassettes are then processed digitally to produce the final image. On the other hand, DR Equipment stands for Digital Radiography equipment that directly captures X-ray images using electronic detectors, eliminating the need for cassettes. Both types of equipment are used for security purposes, providing portable X-ray solutions for screening baggage, vehicles, and other objects in high-security environments.</p></p>
