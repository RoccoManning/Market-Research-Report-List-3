<p><p>The vertical laminar flow hoods market includes variants with HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters and ULPA (Ultra-Low Particulate Air) filters. HEPA filters are capable of removing 99.97% of particles with a size of 0.3 micrometers or larger, making them suitable for applications requiring high levels of cleanliness, such as pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. ULPA filters offer even higher filtration efficiency, capturing 99.999% of 0.12 micrometer particles, and are typically used in ultra-critical environments, such as semiconductor manufacturing and nanotechnology research. Both types ensure a clean and sterile working area by maintaining a controlled airflow.</p></p>
