<p><p>Boxboards are a type of packaging material commonly used in the production of corrugated cardboard. There are two main types of boxboards on the market: Testliner and Kraftliner. Testliner is made from recycled paper and is often used for the inner layer of corrugated cardboard. Kraftliner, on the other hand, is made from virgin pulp and is typically used for the outer layer of corrugated cardboard. Both Testliner and Kraftliner have their own distinct advantages and are used in different applications within the packaging industry.</p></p>
