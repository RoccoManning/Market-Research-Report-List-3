<p><p>A Three Axis Ring Laser Gyroscope (RLG) is a precision instrument used to measure angular velocity in three axes. It is widely used in various applications such as navigation systems, aerospace and defense, robotics, and industrial automation. The RLG technology is based on the Sagnac effect, where a laser beam is split into two counter-propagating beams that travel in a closed loop. The difference in the phase shift between the two beams is used to measure the rotational motion.</p><p>The current outlook of the Three Axis Ring Laser Gyroscope (RLG) market is positive, with an expected CAGR of 6.5% during the forecasted period.
