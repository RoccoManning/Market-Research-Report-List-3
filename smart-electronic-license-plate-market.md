<p><p>Smart Electronic License Plate market includes electronic tags and monitoring base stations (card readers). Electronic tags are wireless devices attached to vehicles to allow for automatic identification and tracking. Monitoring base stations, or card readers, are stationary units that communicate with the electronic tags to record vehicle information and track their movements. These two components work together to create a comprehensive system for monitoring and managing vehicle traffic efficiently and accurately.</p></p>
