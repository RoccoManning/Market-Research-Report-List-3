<p><p>Auto suspension system market consists of two main types - Independent auto suspension system and Non-independent auto suspension system. Independent suspension system allows each wheel to move independently, providing a smoother ride and better handling. On the other hand, Non-independent suspension system connects both wheels on an axle, causing movement in one wheel to affect the other. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
