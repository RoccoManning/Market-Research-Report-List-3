<p><p>Swim training fins are available in three main types: short blade swim fins, long blade swim fins, and monofins. Short blade swim fins have a smaller surface area, promoting faster kicking movements, perfect for sprint training. Long blade swim fins provide more resistance, helping build leg strength and power suitable for endurance training. Monofins are a single-blade swim fin that mimics a dolphin's tail, enabling a unique and efficient swimming technique. Each type caters to different training needs and goals, allowing swimmers to enhance specific aspects of their swimming abilities.</p></p>
