<p><p>Self-sealing envelopes are a type of envelope that close automatically without the need for adhesive or moisture. They have an adhesive strip on the flap which, when licked or pressed, activates the gum and securely seals the envelope. These envelopes are commonly used for sending official documents, invoices, and other important mailings. They offer convenience, time-saving, and security advantages compared to traditional envelopes that require moistening the gum or using adhesives.</p><p>The current outlook for the self-sealing envelopes market is positive and shows potential for growth. The increasing demand for efficient packaging solutions and the rising use of self-sealing envelopes in various industries, including e-commerce, banking, and logistics, are key factors driving market growth.
