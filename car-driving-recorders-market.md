<p><p>Car driving recorders, also known as dashcams, come in two main market types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel dashcams are designed to record footage from one direction, typically the front of the vehicle. They are popular for individual drivers who are mainly concerned with capturing incidents that occur in front of their vehicle. On the other hand, multi-channel dashcams are equipped with multiple lenses to capture footage from multiple angles, providing more comprehensive coverage for commercial vehicles or drivers who want to capture footage from all directions.</p></p>
