<p><p>Non-woven polypropylene fiber market types are categorized based on melt flow rate (MFR). MFR is the measure of the molten polypropylene flow through a specific die over a period of time. The types include <25 min, 25-40 g/10 min, and >40 g/10 min. Lower MFR indicates higher viscosity, suitable for applications requiring strength and durability. In contrast, higher MFR types are preferred for applications where flexibility and softness are key, such as in hygiene products and medical textiles.</p></p>
