<p><p>Conference projectors market is divided into three main types. The first type includes projectors with VGA interfaces, which use the traditional video graphics array technology to connect to devices. The second type comprises HDMI projectors, which utilize high-definition multimedia interface for enhanced audio and video quality. The third type consists of network port projectors, capable of connecting to local area networks for convenient control and sharing of multimedia content. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of users in the conference setting.</p></p>
