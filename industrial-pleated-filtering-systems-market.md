<p><p>Industrial Pleated Filtering Systems Market consists of three main types based on their Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) ratings. MERV 10 and Below filters offer basic filtration for capturing larger particles. MERV 12 to 14 filters provide medium-efficiency filtration, capturing smaller particles like mold spores and dust. MERV 14 and Above filters offer high-efficiency filtration and are suitable for environments requiring superior air quality, such as hospitals or clean rooms. Each type of filter caters to specific filtration needs and air quality requirements.</p></p>
