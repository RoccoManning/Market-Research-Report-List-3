<p><p>A Laser Distance Meter is a handheld device used to accurately measure distances, with the help of laser technology. It emits a laser beam and measures the time it takes for the laser to bounce back from the target object to calculate the distance. Laser distance meters are widely used in various industries such as construction, engineering, architecture, and real estate for precise distance measurements.</p><p>The Laser Distance Meter Market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of laser technology in various industries.
