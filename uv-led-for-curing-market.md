<p><p>UV LED for curing market can be categorized into three main types based on the wavelength of the ultraviolet light emitted: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LED lights emit long-wave ultraviolet light and are commonly used for curing inks, coatings, and adhesives. UV-B LED lights emit medium-wave ultraviolet light and are used for applications such as phototherapy and medical sterilization. UV-C LED lights emit short-wave ultraviolet light and are mainly used for disinfection and water purification purposes. Each type of UV LED serves specific industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
