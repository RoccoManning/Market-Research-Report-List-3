<p><p>The work gloves market can be categorized into two types: disposable gloves and reusable gloves. They are commonly used in industries where hygiene is crucial, such as healthcare and food processing. On the other hand, reusable gloves are made from durable materials like leather or cotton and can be washed and reused multiple times. They are suitable for industries such as construction, manufacturing, and gardening, where protection and longevity are important.</p></p>
