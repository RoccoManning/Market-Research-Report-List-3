<p><p>Bluetooth ceiling speakers are classified into two types: active and passive. Active Bluetooth ceiling speakers have built-in amplifiers and do not require an external amplifier to function. They can directly connect to devices like smartphones or tablets via Bluetooth for audio playback. On the other hand, passive Bluetooth ceiling speakers rely on an external amplifier to power and control the audio signal. They can be used in larger audio systems where multiple speakers need to be connected to the amplifier for a synchronized audio experience.</p></p>
