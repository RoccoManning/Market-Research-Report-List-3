<p><strong>The Automatic Checkweigher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic checkweigher market encompasses two main types of checkweighers: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers are designed to measure the weight of products as they move along a conveyor belt, allowing for continuous and high-speed weighing processes. On the other hand, intermittent checkweighers are used for batch weighing, where products are weighed in groups or at specific intervals. Both types of checkweighers are utilized in various industries to ensure accuracy in product weight and meet quality control standards.</p></p>
