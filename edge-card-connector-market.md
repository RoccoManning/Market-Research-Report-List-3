<p><p>An edge card connector is a type of electrical connector that is used to connect an electronic device, such as a printed circuit board (PCB), to an external device or another PCB. It is called an edge card connector because it has contacts or pins on one or both sides that connect to the edge of the PCB.</p><p>The market for edge card connectors is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for high-speed internet and data transfer has led to the need for advanced and efficient connectors that can handle high-frequency signals with low signal loss. Edge card connectors provide this capability and are widely used in industries such as telecommunications, aerospace, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p><p>Moreover, the growing adoption of cloud computing and the Internet of Things (IoT) is also driving the demand for edge card connectors. These connectors play a crucial role in connecting devices and enabling seamless communication between them.
