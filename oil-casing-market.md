<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The oil casing market includes various types of casings used in oil drilling operations. Surface casing is the outermost layer, providing structural support and preventing the well from collapsing. Technical casing is used in the middle section to isolate different formations and protect the wellbore's integrity. Oil layer casing, also known as production casing, is installed in the deepest section to extract oil and gas. These casings help maintain well stability, prevent leakage, and safeguard the environment during drilling and production processes.</p></p>
