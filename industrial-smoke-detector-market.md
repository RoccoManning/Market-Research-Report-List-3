<p><p>The industrial smoke detector market consists of three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and dual sensor smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light beam to detect smoke particles, making them effective at detecting slow, smoldering fires. Ionization smoke alarms use a small amount of radioactive material to ionize the air, detecting fast, flaming fires. Dual sensor smoke alarms combine both technologies for enhanced fire detection capabilities. These different types cater to various fire scenarios by offering distinct methods of smoke detection.</p></p>
