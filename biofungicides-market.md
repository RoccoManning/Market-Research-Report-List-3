<p><ul><li>Dry Formulation</li><li>Liquid Formulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biofungicides are classified into two types based on formulation: dry formulation and liquid formulation. Dry formulation biofungicides are powdered or granular products that are easy to handle and store. They are often applied by mixing with soil or using seed treatment methods. Liquid formulation biofungicides are liquid concentrates that are diluted with water before application. They are generally easier to apply and provide better coverage on plant surfaces. Both types of biofungicides offer effective protection against fungal diseases in crops.</p></p>
