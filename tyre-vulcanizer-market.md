<p><p>The Tyre Vulcanizer market is divided into two main types - Mechanical Vulcanizer and Hydraulic Vulcanizer. Mechanical Vulcanizers are a type of equipment used for curing (heating and pressurizing) the tire in order to give it its final shape and strength. These machines operate using a mechanical system, typically powered by electricity. On the other hand, Hydraulic Vulcanizers utilize hydraulic power to provide the necessary heat and pressure for the curing process. Both types of vulcanizers play a crucial role in the tire manufacturing industry, ensuring the production of durable and safe tires.</p></p>
