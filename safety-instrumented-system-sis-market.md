<p><p>A Safety Instrumented System (SIS) is a vital component in industries where safety is a priority. The SIS market consists of three main types: sensor, logic solver, and final element. Sensors detect abnormal conditions and send signals to the logic solver, which processes the information and initiates the appropriate response. The final elements carry out the required action, such as closing a valve or activating an alarm. Each market type plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and protection of industrial processes and personnel.</p></p>
