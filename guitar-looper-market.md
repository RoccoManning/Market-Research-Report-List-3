<p><p>The guitar looper market can be divided into two main types: floor-based and desktop. Floor-based loopers are pedal-like units that can be placed on the floor and controlled by foot. They often come with additional footswitches for easy control during performances. On the other hand, desktop loopers are compact units designed to sit on a desk or tabletop. They usually offer similar functionalities as floor-based loopers but are more suitable for home recording or studio use. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of guitar players in terms of convenience and usage.</p></p>
