C Blades have a curved shape that helps in chopping crop residues and turning the soil effectively. J Blades, on the other hand, have a more aggressive angle and can dig deeper into the ground, making them ideal for tough terrain. These different blade types offer varying benefits and cater to specific needs of farmers when using rotavators for soil preparation and cultivation.</p></p>
