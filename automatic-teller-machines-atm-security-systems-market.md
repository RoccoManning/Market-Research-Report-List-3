<p><p>The Automatic Teller Machines (ATM) security systems market primarily consists of three types: surveillance systems, alarm systems, and control systems. Surveillance systems utilize cameras and recording equipment to monitor the ATM area, deterring criminal activity and providing evidence if incidents occur. Alarm systems consist of sensors and alarms that trigger when unauthorized access or tampering is detected, notifying security personnel or law enforcement. Control systems manage access permissions, ensuring only authorized individuals can operate the ATM and protecting against theft or vandalism. These systems work together to enhance the security of ATMs and safeguard the users' transactions and personal information.</p></p>
