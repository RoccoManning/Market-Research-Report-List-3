<p><p>Automotive suspension market consists of various types including coil springs, leaf springs, stabilizer bars, suspension arms, and other components. Coil springs are cylindrical springs that absorb shock and support the weight of the vehicle. Leaf springs are long strips of metal that provide support and stability. Stabilizer bars reduce body roll during cornering. Suspension arms connect the suspension system to the chassis. Other components include shock absorbers, struts, and control arms, all working together to ensure a smooth and controlled ride for vehicles.</p></p>
