<p><p>The ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) Driving Control Unit market consists of three main types: Perception Control Unit, Decision Control Unit, and Actuator Control Unit. The Perception Control Unit gathers data from various sensors, such as cameras and radar, to detect and understand the surroundings of the vehicle. The Decision Control Unit processes this data, making intelligent decisions and issuing commands for specific actions. The Actuator Control Unit receives these commands and controls the actuators, such as brakes and steering, to perform the desired actions, ensuring a safe and efficient driving experience.</p></p>
