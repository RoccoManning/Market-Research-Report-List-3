<p><p>Sand filters are a type of water filtration system commonly used in various industries and applications. There are two main types: vertical media filters and horizontal media filters. Vertical media filters have a vertical design, where water flows from top to bottom through a bed of sand or other filtering media. Horizontal media filters, on the other hand, have a horizontal design, with water passing through the sand or filtering media from one side to the other. Both types effectively remove impurities and contaminants from water, ensuring clean and safe filtration.</p></p>
