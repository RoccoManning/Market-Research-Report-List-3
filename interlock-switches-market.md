<p><p>Interlock switches are safety devices used in various industries to prevent accidents and injuries. Non-contact interlock switches utilize proximity sensors to detect the presence of an object, ensuring that machines or equipment cannot be operated when a person is in the danger zone. Tongue interlock switches are physical switches that require a specific key or tongue to be inserted in order to activate or deactivate the equipment. Other types of interlock switches include hinge switches, which use rotational motion to activate the switch, and cable-operated switches, which are activated by pulling or releasing a cable.</p></p>
