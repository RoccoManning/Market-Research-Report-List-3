<p><p>The automotive antenna module market consists of three main types: fin type, rod type, and screen type. Fin type antennas are sleek and typically integrated into the roof of vehicles, offering better reception and aesthetic appeal. Rod type antennas are external and are commonly seen on older car models, providing reliable reception but with less refined appearance. Screen type antennas are embedded within vehicle windows, ensuring good signal strength and unobtrusive design. These different types cater to diverse consumer preferences, combining functionality and visual appeal in automotive communication systems.</p></p>
