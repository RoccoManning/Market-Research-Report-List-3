<p><p>The cable sleeves market offers various types of sleeves to meet different needs. Tubular sleeves are cylindrical and serve as a basic protective cover. Braided sleeves are flexible and tightly woven, providing superior abrasion resistance. Corrugated sleeves have a ridged design, allowing for flexibility and protection in confined spaces. Spiral sleeves are helical and offer excellent wire management by grouping multiple cables. Finally, other sleeves encompass niche products like heat shrink tubes, expandable mesh, and cotton cloth sleeves. Each sleeve type serves a specific purpose in organizing and safeguarding cables.</p></p>
