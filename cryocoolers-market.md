<p><p>Cryocoolers market comprises several types of cooling systems used in various applications. Gifford-McMahon cryocoolers utilize a compressor to cool materials below -150Â°C. Pulse-tube cryocoolers are based on the principle of oscillating gas within a tube, achieving cryogenic temperatures. Stirling cryocoolers rely on a piston-cylinder assembly driven by an external motor to compress, expand, and cool gases. Joule-Thomson cryocoolers utilize a throttling process to cool gases. Brayton cryocoolers use a gas cycle involving a compressor, heat exchanger, and turbine to achieve cryogenic temperatures. Each type has unique advantages and applications in industries such as electronics, medicine, and aerospace.</p></p>
