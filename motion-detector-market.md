<p><p>The motion detector market consists of different types of sensors including accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, and combo sensor. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration and are commonly used in applications such as tilt detection and impact monitoring. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity and are used for tasks like motion tracking and stabilization. Magnetometers detect magnetic fields and are utilized in compass applications. Combo sensors incorporate multiple sensor technologies to offer a comprehensive solution for motion detection, combining the functionality of accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer sensors. These sensors find applications in various industries ranging from consumer electronics to automotive and healthcare.</p></p>
