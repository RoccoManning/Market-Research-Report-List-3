<p><p>Flexible dies are thin and flexible cutting tools made from a solid sheet of steel ruled with a series of very fine and precise knives. The market is also experiencing an increasing demand for customized dies to cater to specific requirements of industries such as automotive, aerospace, and healthcare.</p><p>In conclusion, the flexible dies market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing demand for efficient die-cutting solutions and advancements in printing technology.
