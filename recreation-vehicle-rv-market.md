<p><p>Recreation Vehicles (RVs) can be classified into two main market types: motorized RVs and towable RVs. Motorized RVs are self-contained units that include a motor and can be driven independently, such as Class A, B, and C motorhomes. Towable RVs, on the other hand, need to be towed by another vehicle, like travel trailers, fifth wheel trailers, and pop-up campers. Both types of RVs cater to different preferences and needs of consumers looking for a mobile living or camping experience.</p></p>
