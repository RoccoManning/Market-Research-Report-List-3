Load-lock Etching Systems allow for quick loading and unloading of wafers without exposing the main chamber to outside air, resulting in improved process stability and reduced contamination. Cassette-load Etching Systems use cassettes to hold multiple wafers, enabling high-throughput processing. Both systems offer advanced capabilities for precise etching of silicon wafers and are essential tools in semiconductor fabrication facilities.</p></p>
