<p><p>Automotive crankshaft sensors are crucial for monitoring engine speed and position. There are four main types in the market: Hall Effect sensors, Magneto Resistive sensors, Inductive sensors, and Optical sensors. Hall Effect sensors use the principle of electromagnetic induction, Magneto Resistive sensors work based on changes in resistance, Inductive sensors detect metal passing through a magnetic field, and Optical sensors utilize light to detect movement. Each type has its own advantages and applications in automotive systems.</p></p>
