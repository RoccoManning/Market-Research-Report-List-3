<p><p>Shuttle blister packaging systems are available in three different market types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. In the manual market, the packaging process is done entirely by hand, with operators manually placing the products into the blister packs and sealing them. In the semi-automatic market, some of the steps are automated, such as blister forming, while others still require manual intervention. Finally, in the automatic market, the entire process is fully automated, including blister forming, product placement, and sealing, reducing the need for human involvement and increasing efficiency.</p></p>
