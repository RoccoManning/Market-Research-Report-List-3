<p><p>Metal food and beverage packaging cans come in two main types: two-pieces can and three-pieces can. In a two-pieces can, the body and bottom are created from a single metal sheet, which is formed into a cylinder shape. The top lid is then sealed onto the can. On the other hand, a three-pieces can is made up of a separate body, bottom, and top lid. The body and bottom are joined together, and the top lid is sealed separately. Both types of cans offer secure and hygienic packaging for food and beverages.</p></p>
