<p><p>Packaged air handling units (AHUs) are pre-engineered and fully assembled air handling systems that are used to regulate and circulate air within commercial and industrial buildings. The market is expected to witness significant demand due to factors such as increasing focus on energy-efficient HVAC systems, growing construction activities, and the need for better indoor air quality.
