<p><p>Ducting silencers are devices used to reduce noise generated by the flow of air or gases in ductwork systems. There are several types of ducting silencers available in the market. Rectangular silencers are designed to be installed in rectangular ducts and provide effective noise attenuation. Elbow silencers are specifically made to reduce noise produced at the bends and corners of ductwork. Circular silencers are commonly used in round ducts to minimize noise. Apart from these types, there are other variations available in the market, each designed to address specific noise reduction requirements in different types of ductwork systems.</p></p>
