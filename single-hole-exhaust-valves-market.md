<p><p>Single hole exhaust valves are available in two main types: Flange Type and Thread Type. Flange Type exhaust valves are designed to be mounted onto a flange for easy installation and removal. They are commonly used in industrial applications where high pressure and temperature are often present. Thread Type exhaust valves, on the other hand, have threads that allow them to be screwed directly into a pipe or exhaust system. They are more commonly used in smaller applications or where space is limited. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
