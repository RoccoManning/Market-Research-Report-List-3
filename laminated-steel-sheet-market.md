<p><ul><li>Fusion Method Laminated Steel</li><li>Bonding Agent Laminated Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminated steel sheet market consists of two main types: fusion method laminated steel and bonding agent laminated steel. Fusion method involves melting a layer of steel onto another steel sheet, creating a strong bond. This type is widely used in industries such as automotive and construction. On the other hand, bonding agent laminated steel involves using a bonding agent to adhere layers of steel together. This type is commonly used in electrical appliances and household goods. Both types offer durability and enhanced strength, catering to various applications in different markets.</p></p>
