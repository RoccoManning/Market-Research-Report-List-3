<p><p>The Semiconductor Inspection System market is divided into two types: the Wafer Semiconductor Inspection System and the Mask Semiconductor Inspection System. The Wafer Semiconductor Inspection System is designed to inspect the surface quality, pattern, and defects of wafers used in the semiconductor manufacturing process. On the other hand, the Mask Semiconductor Inspection System is used for inspecting the pattern, alignment, and defects on the masks used in lithography processes. Both systems play crucial roles in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor manufacturing, but they focus on different stages of the production process.</p></p>
